Healthy Kitten Food Recipes

Vegan Kitten Food Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup cooked sweet potato, mashed
  • 1/4 cup cooked peas or green beans, mashed
  • 1/4 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • 1 boiled egg, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of tofu, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon flaxseed oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vegan taurine powder
  • 1/4 cup kitten formula or water

Instructions:

  • Cook the sweet potato, peas or green beans, and quinoa or rice until soft and mash.
  • Combine the mashed vegetables and quinoa/rice in a bowl.
  • Add the finely chopped boiled egg, crumbled tofu, nutritional yeast, flaxseed oil, and vegan taurine powder.
  • Gradually add kitten formula or water to reach a soft, moist consistency.
  • Serve small portions at room temperature. Store leftovers in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Goat Milk Kitten Formula Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup goat milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon plain yogurt (unsweetened)
  • 1 teaspoon corn syrup or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon vegan taurine powder

Instructions:

  • In a bowl, combine the goat milk, egg yolk, plain yogurt, corn syrup or maple syrup, nutritional yeast, and vegan taurine powder.
  • Whisk until smooth and well mixed.
  • Store the formula in a sterilized container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
  • Warm to body temperature before feeding.
